01

About

Ten years ago I was fixing typography on other people's blogs for free. Since then I've shipped design for a reading app, two note-taking tools, and a small pile of side projects that mostly taught me what not to build. I care about the unglamorous parts — empty states, error copy, the moment a new person opens the thing for the first time.

These days I work with early-stage teams who want their product to feel considered without feeling precious. When I'm not doing that, I'm usually writing, walking, or arguing with myself about serif choices.
